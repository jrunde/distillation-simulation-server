Bioenergy Sustainability

In this game, players take on the role of an individual farmer that must make yearly decisions about how to best manage his or her fields to reach a predefined long-term objective. These decisions reflect individual player decisions about crop choices and (optionally) management options on individual fields. Players are presented with a range of information on which to base their choices; this information reflects the three components which have been identified as critical to the sustainability of this system and reflect the environmental impact, economics, and energy production that result from player decisions.
