Welcome to the Fields of Fuel help page!

This site serves as both a gameplay tutorial and informational document for Fields of Fuel. The sidebar at left will help you navigate through the information housed in the help section: the links toward the top of the list pertain more to how an instructor would go about using Fields of Fuel as a teaching tool in his or her classroom while the bottom-most links will tend to explain the theory behind the mechanics of the game. The latter content will inevitably touch on some of the key concepts and issues raised by Fields of Fuel.
