Trade-Offs

The game is based on making decisions about tradeoffs between economic, energy production, and environmental benefits. These tradeoffs and their associated severity are summarized in the table below. The number represents the magnitude that the player choice (either crop choice or management options) will have on the corresponding elements of individual fields (yield, soil health, and water quality).
